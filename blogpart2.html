"How Photoshop Reshaped My Pandemic Era"

I didn’t just start Photoshop because I wanted to. It drew my attention to how beautiful graphic designing truly is, especially in esports. During a time when everything felt uncertain and overwhelming, Photoshop became my safe space. At first, it was just for fun—clicking random buttons and experimenting. Pero habang tumatagal, I realized na Photoshop wasn’t just something I used anymore. It slowly became something that shaped who I am today.

This blog is about how Photoshop helped me grow, not just creatively, but personally.

How I learned Photoshop during the Pandemic.

When I first opened Photoshop, I was not ready and I was confused just like any child would be. I made my first edit not knowing that it would be the start of something beautiful. I gave mini-projects to myself and learned everything through self-learning. The designs I created did not make any sense at first, but instead of getting discouraged, I kept trying. Every edit felt like a challenge, and every mistake felt like a lesson.

Photoshop became my way of expressing my true self that I couldn’t explain in words. Every free time I had, I edited. When I felt bored, I started editing. Kahit simple lang ang ginagawa ko, it gave me a purpose. Creating something from nothing filled me with happiness.

  • Watched free Photoshop tutorials online
  • Created mini personal projects
  • Practiced editing during my free time

How and when I started earning money thru editing

I started editing in 2020, and during my first year, I received an invitation to join a small team with a salary of **** PHP. At just 15 years old, I was already earning money, which I immediately accepted. That opportunity introduced me to the real struggles of being a graphic designer. As the team grew bigger in the scene, they began looking for new graphic designers. I eventually left because I felt I couldn’t compete yet, so I decided to improve my skills.

After that, I found another job in the same field esports which also offered a salary of **** PHP. These new teams later became a second home for me. They suggested that I become a live-in editor at a bootcamp house. Their supportive community made everything better for me and their players, which made me very happy and helped shape my journey throughout 2021–2022.

  • Started earning money at the age of 15
  • Experienced real pressure
  • Entered the professional esports editing scene

Who I became because of editing.

Through editing, I slowly discovered who I truly am. It taught me discipline, patience, and confidence in my own skills. I learned how to handle pressure, deadlines, and criticism from other people, which helped me grow not just as a graphic designer but as a person. Editing gave me an identity during a hard time for the whole world. It showed me that passion and hard work can open opportunities, kahit bata pa ako. Because of Photoshop and editing, I became more motivated, more creative, and more determined.

  1. Became more disciplined and patient
  2. Gained confidence in my creativity
  3. Developed a name for myself in the scene

Photoshop became more than just an editing tool for me; it became a part of my life. From learning it during the pandemic, to earning my first income, and discovering who I am through editing, every experience shaped me into a better version of myself. What started as a simple hobby turned into a passion and a source of happiness. Through challenges and struggles, editing taught me valuable lessons. This journey reminded me that even during difficult times, something meaningful can emerge and change your life forever.
