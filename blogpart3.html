"A creative future for my creative mind?"

After everything I’ve been through with Graphic designing, I’ve always put in my mind that my journey doesn’t stop at learning skills or earning money early. What started as a hobby for me slowly turned into a passion, and this shaped the future I want to build. I didn’t always have a clear plan for my career in IT, but with my past years in editing, working with esports teams, and learning from real experiences, I began to see where I truly belong. This blog is about the career I want to pursue, the skills I still need to improve, and how I see myself growing in the next few years.

The career I see myself with.

In the next years, I see myself working professionally in the creative side of the IT industry, especially in graphic design and digital media based companies. I want to be more than just an editor who follows trends. I want to be someone who creates designs with identity. My experience as a graphic designer during a young age taught me that creativity can really open opportunities, and I want to continue building a career where I can combine technology and art.

I also want to expand beyond Photoshop. I know that the IT field is constantly evolving. In the future, I hope to work with different tools, platforms, and even collaborate with developers, or marketing teams. My goal is to become a well-known and the best creative professional who understands both design and technology.

More learning experience to be gained.

Even though I already have experience, I know that I still have a lot to learn. One of the most important skills I need to improve is consistency. Working in esports taught me how fast-paced and demanding the industry can be, and I want to become more disciplined with my time and workflow. I also want to improve my technical and logical skills by learning more advanced design techniques and exploring other software related to media and IT.

Aside from technical and logical skills, communication is very important. Working with teams showed me that being able to understand feedback and work under pressure is just as important as creativity. I want to become more confident in sharing my ideas and collaborating with others. These skills will help me prepare for bigger opportunities in the future.

A past that can help.

Starting my editing journey during the pandemic and earning at a young age gave me a different perspective on work and responsibility. I experienced pressure early, but instead of giving up, it pushed me to improve myself. Those moments made me realize that setbacks are part of growth. Because of that, I am not afraid of challenges that may come in my future career.

My experiences also taught me that passion alone is not enough continuous learning is important. This is why I plan to keep practicing, studying, and improving my skills while pursuing my education. I want to use what I learned from the past to make smarter decisions moving forward.

Develop more. Build more.

As I move forward, I plan to continue developing my skills, exploring new technologies, and building a career that I am proud of. My goal is not just to earn money, but to create meaningful work and leave a mark in the industry I love. Through dedication and continuous learning, I believe I can turn my passion into a long-term career and a better future for myself.
